# Baobab Company Sales Performance Dashboard (Excel)

## Project Overview

This project focuses on operational data analysis and visualization for **Baobab Company**, a wood industry firm. The primary goal was to create an interactive Microsoft Excel dashboard to monitor and analyze sales volume performance against a key company objective: **to increase the monthly sales volume by 5%**.

This dashboard serves as a vital tool for Sales Managers and Data Analysts to quickly identify sales trends, regional contributions, and individual sales representative performance.

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) and Objectives

The analysis and dashboard were designed to track the following metrics required by the business:

1.  **Total Sales Volume:** Overall performance tracking.
2.  **Monthly Sales Units:** Tracking the month-over-month trend to assess the 5% growth objective.
3.  **Sales Volume by Region:** Identifying top-performing and underperforming geographic areas.
4.  **Average Products Purchased per Customer:** Analyzing customer engagement and order value.
5.  **Sales Units by Product Category and Sales Rep:** Evaluating sales representative efficiency and product specialization.

## Technology and Methodology

### 1. Tools Used
* **Microsoft Excel:** Primary tool for data manipulation, analysis (Pivot Tables), and visualization.
* **Excel Functions:** Heavy utilization of `VLOOKUP` / `INDEX/MATCH` to consolidate disparate data sources.
* **Pivot Tables & Slicers:** Used for dynamic data aggregation and interactive filtering on the dashboard.

### 2. Data Preparation (Data Wrangling)
The project required consolidating data from four separate source tables into a single Master Sales Table (`Prodaja 1`). This step was crucial to establish relationships between sales transactions, product details, customer information, and sales representative data.

### 3. Dashboard Structure
The final dashboard is structured to provide an immediate view of the most critical sales figures, organized across the following charts:
* Total Sales Volume Card
* Sales Volume Trend (Monthly Bar Chart)
* Regional Performance (Bar Chart)
* Average Customer Order Size (Bar Chart)
* Sales Unit Distribution by Category (Donut/Pie Chart)

## Key Insights and Business Recommendations

Based on the operational analysis, the following insights and recommendations were generated:

* **Regional Focus:** Identified **Srbija** as the leading region in sales volume. Recommendation: Investigate the strategies used in this high-performing region for replication in others (e.g., Vojvodina and Beogradski region).
* **Growth Tracking:** Observed a clear **month-over-month increase** in units sold (Jan < Feb < Mar). Recommendation: Maintain the current sales momentum and project future growth requirements to hit the 5% monthly target consistently.
* **Performance Optimization:** The cross-analysis of categories and sales representatives indicates areas for specialized training. Recommendation: Implement targeted training programs where sales reps are weakest, focusing on boosting volume in underperforming categories.
* **Customer Engagement:** Monitored the **Average Products Purchased per Customer** trend to understand changes in customer buying behavior over time.
